SPECIFICATIONS

Structure

  • Masonry - Aluminium form work CC wall
  • Painting - Internal painting in premium emulsion. External painting in quality water proof emulsion
  • Parapets - A combination of Stainless steel and toughened laminated glass

SANITARY & PLUMBING

  • Sanitaryware - EWC's and washbasins of TOTO / equivalent make in bathrooms
  • Cisterns - TOTO/ equivalent make water saving, dual flush concealed cisterns in all bathrooms
  • Health Faucets - TOTO / Shower Spray, Chrome. 4.0 LPM (GREEN ITEM) / equivalent make
  • Cockroach Traps - Chilly / equivalent make detachable stainless steel cockroach traps
  • Shower Cubicles - Glass shower partitions / cubicles for shower areas in all bathrooms
  • Overhead Shower - Rain shower of TOTO / equivalent make in master bedroom. Shower of TOTO / equivalent make in all bathrooms
  • Faucets - Chromium plated heavy body metal faucets of TOTO / equivalent make in all bathrooms
  • Counters - Quartz / equivalent vanity counters in all bathrooms
  • Utility - Granite counter sink in utility

FLOORING & DADO STRUCTURES

  • Living & Dining - Quality ltalian Marble laid with paper joints and finished with mirror polish
  • Balcony - Wood finish designer tiles
  • Master Bedroom - Superior grade composite wooden flooring
  • Other Bedroom - Designer vitrified tiles
  • Bathrooms - Choice of anti skid superior vitrified tiles / porcelain and glass mosaic tiles on the floor and dado
  • Kitchen & Utility Flooring - Anti stain designer tiles
  • Kitchen - Supply of dado above platform
  • Lobbies & Corridors - Marble / Granite / Tiles

DOORS & WINDOWS

  • Door Frames - Teakwood for main door. Hardwood frame for internal / other doors
  • Door Shutters - The main door is teak wood. Designer veneered doors for all other /internal doors
  • Windows - Environment friendly, flame retardant UPVC windows / equivalent
  • Door Stoppers - Magnetic door stoppers shall be provided for the main door and bedroom doors

ELECTRICAL & TELECOM

  • Wiring - Fire retardant multi-strand copper wires will be used throughout with concealed PVC conduits
  • Lighting - Generous number of light point provisions in all other habitable places
  • Power Backup - DG backup for all homes

HOME SECURITY SYSTEMS

  • Networked Video Door phones - A state of the art networked color video door phone
  • Smoke and Heat Detector - Provided in common areas
  • Power Outlets - WIFI point in living area
  • IP Camera - High resolution network cameras shall be provided in designated common areas

SERVICES

  • Elevators - Five high speed passenger elevators of Mitsubishi / equivalent brand. Elevators will have multi-beam sensors for door operation and down collective system
  • Water Treatment Plant - Fully treated water made available through and exclusive water softening and purification plant
  • Sewage Treatment Plant - A tertiary sewage treatment plant with separate plumbing will enable the usage of treated water
  • Rainwater Harvesting - Rainwater harvesting to water treatment plant and through percolation pits for recharging the groundwater table
